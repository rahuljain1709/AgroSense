Coconut thrives in coastal and humid tropical regions. 
It grows well in deep sandy loam soils with adequate drainage. 
Regular watering during dry periods is important for nut production. 
Coconut palms need balanced fertilization and benefit greatly from organic matter and mulching. 
Proper spacing and sunlight exposure improve flowering and nut set. 
Intercropping with spices and banana is common in coconut plantations.
