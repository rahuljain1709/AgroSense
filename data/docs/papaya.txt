Papaya is a fast-growing tropical fruit crop that prefers warm temperatures and high humidity. 
It thrives in well-drained loamy soils rich in organic matter. 
Papaya is highly sensitive to waterlogging and root rot. 
Male-to-female plant ratio is important for fruit set in dioecious varieties. 
Regular nutrient application and irrigation help maintain vigorous growth. 
Papaya is prone to viral diseases; rogueing infected plants is necessary.
