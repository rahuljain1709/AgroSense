Maize is a versatile cereal crop grown in various seasons depending on the region. 
It prefers well-drained loamy soils and moderate rainfall. 
Maize is sensitive to standing water and requires proper field drainage. 
Balanced fertilization, especially nitrogen applied in splits, is important. 
Timely sowing and weed-free conditions during the early stages are essential. 
Maize responds well to irrigation at critical growth stages like tasseling and silking.
