Pigeonpea is a long-duration pulse crop tolerant to drought and heat. 
It prefers well-drained loamy soils and moderate rainfall. 
The deep root system helps pigeonpea survive dry periods. 
As a legume, it fixes nitrogen and improves soil fertility. 
Proper spacing ensures better branching and pod development. 
Pigeonpea is sensitive to waterlogging during early growth stages.
